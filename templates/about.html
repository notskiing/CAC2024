We are Siddharth and Saanvi Kohli. We're twins and juniors in high school. We created swapz after realizing how much waste the fashion industry creates in the world around us, and after we realized we needed to use the resources we have to make a change.

Our app was made to reduce the amount of waste which fast fashion has in our community. It helps to reduce this by creating a safe, secure platform where people can easily trade their clothing items with others for a more sustainable community in general. The target audience is mostly for high school students after their dances, or any of our Northern California community members who want to spice up their wardrobes! We plan on spreading word of mouth by posting QR codes to this site (when live) at our local libraries, schools, and other public locations.
